BINARY SEARCH TREE
- store items ordered by their keys
- search items (based on rules) by their keys
- sort data by keys < (left) root > (right)
- searching takes time proportional to log2n( n# nodes)

HASH TABLE
- associative arrays, hash maps map
- store items that can be found by their key
- search for items by their key
- goal: the time to search a key/item should be independent of n
